Quick Start Guide

Proposal Pack

Find prospects, audit their websites, and generate branded proposals. Three decisions, everything else is automated.

Getting Started

Installation

You need four things before your first run:

✓
Claude Code
✓
Python 3.x
✓
Node.js
✓
Chrome / Chromium

Then install dependencies and configure your profile:

pip install -r requirements.txt
claude
> /setup

The setup wizard asks for your agency name, services, brand colors, and default pricing. Takes about 2 minutes. You only do this once.

The Main Workflow

/findaclient

This is the one command that does everything. Open Claude Code and run:

> /findaclient

Here's what happens:

You decide
Pick a business category
Choose from suggestions based on your profile (e.g., "Plumbers in Austin, TX") or type your own.
Automated
Prospects are found and ranked
Web search finds 5 local businesses and scans their websites for opportunity signals. Results are ranked by number of gaps.
You decide
Pick a prospect
The top prospect is recommended. Pick it or choose another from the list.
Automated
Deep research runs
Business dossier, competitor analysis, and detailed opportunity signals are compiled.
Automated
Brand audit runs
Up to 5 pages analyzed for voice, visual identity, SEO, and conversion signals. Each dimension scored.
Automated
Lighthouse audit runs
Performance, accessibility, best practices, and SEO scores. Core Web Vitals measured.
You decide
Pick a pricing package
Your default is pre-selected. Accept it or choose a different template.
Automated
Everything generates
Branded PDF proposal, sales brief with call script, and follow-up email. All personalized with real audit data.
Total time
About 4-5 minutes. You make 3 decisions. The system handles everything else.
What You Get

Output Files

Every run generates these files in the output/ folder:

File What It Is What To Do
brief-[name].md Contact info, internal briefing, call script Read first. Use for the call.
email-[name].md Personalized follow-up email Copy into your email client.
proposal-[name].pdf Branded client-facing proposal Attach to the email.
proposal-[name].html Interactive web version Optional: host or share a link.
proposal-[name].md Markdown version Reference or edit as needed.
After The Run

Your Next Steps

Open the sales brief
Review the contact info, pain points, and call script. The brief tells you exactly what to say and why.
Make the call
Use the call script. Lead with their specific problems, not your services. The 15-second opener gets you in the door.
Send the email
Open the email draft, paste it into your email client, attach the PDF proposal, and send.
Follow up in 3 days
If no response, send a short follow-up. The audit data doesn't expire.
