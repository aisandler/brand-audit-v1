05

Findings

Top 3 Strengths
01
Distinctive, consistent brand voice
The copy reads like a person talking. "No agency. No retainer. A marketing system that compounds." That line does more positioning work than most entire about pages. The anti-agency framing is sharp and runs through every page.
02
Unusually thorough schema markup
Organization, WebSite, and Service schemas with SearchAction and SpeakableSpecification. Most small businesses have zero structured data. This site has three interconnected types with proper @id references. Enterprise-grade technical SEO on a founder-led product.
03
Agent eval scores as built-in proof
Listing each agent with a rubric-graded quality score (avg 91% across 45 test cases) answers the "does this actually work?" objection with data. It's a trust mechanism most AI tools never attempt.
Top 3 Quick Wins
01
Fix the blog page
The /blog route renders no visible content. The title tag uses "Strategic Insights | AI-Era Business Transformation," language that contradicts the brand voice everywhere else. Populate it with posts or remove it from navigation. A dead page with corporate meta copy hurts more than no blog at all.
02
Upgrade testimonials to product outcomes
Five of six testimonials praise webinars or training sessions ("great session," "fantastic teacher"). Only Brad K. speaks to actual product results. For a $147 product, buyers want to hear what the system produced. Even one testimonial with a specific metric would outperform all six current quotes.
03
Strengthen /about title and meta description
Current title is "The Viable Edge | Marketing System" (36 characters, generic). Meta description is 74 characters, under-optimized. Rewrite to match the homepage's specificity with the founder angle and the "own your system" positioning.
